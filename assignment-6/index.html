Welcome to Tattes Bakery

At Tatte Bakery, we savor the perfect cup of coffee, the finest tea, and exceptional food. We treasure the calm of a cozy café, where time slows down and the world fades into the background. Beyond that, we thrive on discovering fresh tastes, connecting with new faces, and embarking on journeys to inspiring places.


What does “tatte” mean?

Tatte — which rhymes with “latte” — is a Hebrew nickname for “grandmother.” Growing up in the kibbutz in Israel, with a family that loved baking, our founder wanted to build the same community and sense of belonging that she always found through baking alongside her mother. While thinking of a logo for the very first farmer’s market stand where she sold her now infamous cookie boxes, her then-18-month-old daughter happened to point to a picture of her grandmother and said, “Tatte, Tatte!” From there, Tatte was born and the rest is delicious history.
